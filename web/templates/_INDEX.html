A tool to help you manage your native seed collection.

Samples are a single collection of seeds for a particular species obtained from a particular source.

Sources are the place where the seed was collected. It could also be the vendor where you purchased seeds. This is used to track the provenance of the seed.

Projects are groups of seed samples intended for a specific purpose, say a planting event or a restoration project.
